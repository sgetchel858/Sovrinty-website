A · The walkthrough

See what happens when someone asks and the answer isn’t ready.

You answer for the knowledge your business runs on, in board reports, in decisions, and in what your AI agents tell people. Walk the path from a question it can’t yet answer to a cited answer anyone can stand behind. Every step links out to the problem it solves.

Scene 0

What happens when your knowledge isn’t ready?

Start from your own situation. Where does your knowledge live, who keeps it current, and how much of it has gone stale? The risk begins the moment an answer goes out that no one verified, whether a person sends it or an AI agent does, and someone acts on it. That answer carries your name.

Scene 1

One source of truth across your tools

Your knowledge is scattered across SharePoint, Box, and shared drives, and no two systems agree. Sovrinty connects to your sources where they live and governs them as one trusted view your people and your AI agents draw from. Everything that follows rests on this.

Subject-matter expert Scene 3

The SME’s desk

Your subject-matter experts are the bottleneck, and they know it. Sovrinty hands them a queue ranked by what matters most, and they clear it without leaving Teams or Slack. The review that used to wait weeks happens in the flow of their day, and the answer everyone depends on gets better.

Scene 4

Proof on every answer

This is the part you answer for. Every answer carries a direct citation, and the full exchange is written to an immutable audit trail. That is the sourcing transparency the general-purpose models can’t give you. Where a regulator requires exact wording, that passage is locked and reproduced word for word. When the auditor asks where an answer came from, you have it.

Admin Scene 5

Stays current, and fits your stack

A new document is published upstream, an Admin approves the change, and the next answer draws from the updated source. Nobody is acting on yesterday’s truth. It runs alongside the AI you already have in place, whether that is Claude, OpenAI, or Copilot.

Everyday user Scene 6

Ask anything

Your people and your AI agents ask a real question and get a trusted, cited answer in seconds. Each answer is gated to who is cleared to see it, and nothing leaves your boundary to get there. It works in Teams and Slack too, so they ask where they already are.
