Problem P6 · Sovereignty

“Our answers can’t leave our environment.”

Where it shows up

Your most sensitive data is what your people and your AI tools most need to work from, and what you can least afford to see leave your walls. The moment a question or its answer touches an outside service, you lose the assurance that nothing left your environment, and that is the assurance your regulators and your board expect you to keep.

What Sovrinty does

Sovrinty is sovereign by design. Questions and answers stay inside your environment, with zero exfiltration as the default and air-gapped or on-prem deployment where you need it. You can put your most sensitive data to work and still tell a regulator, truthfully, that none of it ever left your boundary.
