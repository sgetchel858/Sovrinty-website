Problem P4 · Chasing the SME

“Once I find the SME, I spend all my time chasing them.”

In review → Approved

Where it shows up

A decision waits on the one person who can confirm the answer is right, and that person is in meetings all week. So the answer ships unverified, or the work stalls. Either way, there is no record of who approved it or whether anyone did.

What Sovrinty does

Your SME reviews and approves right where they already work, in Teams or Slack, with no portal to learn and no inbox to chase. They move a source from in review to approved once, and it flows everywhere it is needed. You get the answer faster, and a record of who stood behind it.
