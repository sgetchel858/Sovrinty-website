“Two approved sources disagree and the AI hides it.”

Approved ≠ Approved

Where it shows up

Two approved sources disagree, and your AI smooths the difference into one clean-sounding answer. The disagreement was the signal, and now it is buried. Someone acts on the blended answer, and the contradiction surfaces later in an audit or an incident, when it is far more expensive to explain.

What Sovrinty does

When two approved sources disagree, Sovrinty surfaces the conflict and holds the two answers apart. It routes the disagreement to an SME, and a named expert makes the call. The conflict is surfaced, not averaged into one confident, wrong answer.
