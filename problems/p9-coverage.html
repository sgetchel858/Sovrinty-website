Problem P9 · Coverage

“I can’t see what we don’t know.”

Where it shows up

A question your people keep asking that approved knowledge can’t answer doesn’t announce itself. It hides in low-confidence responses and the workarounds people improvise until the gap costs you a decision or an audit finding. You can’t aim expert effort at a blind spot you can’t see.

What Sovrinty does

Every unanswered and low-confidence query becomes a point on a coverage map of where approved knowledge is missing. You see your blind spots ranked by how often people hit them. Aim expert effort at the ones that matter most, and close them on purpose.
