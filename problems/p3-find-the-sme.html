Problem P3 · Find the SME

“I can’t tell who’s qualified or available to review this.”

In review

Where it shows up

A record lands in review and someone has to find a qualified reviewer. The work drifts to whoever is loudest or nearest, sits in the queue of someone already buried, or waits while a name gets tracked down. Every hour it stalls, an unverified answer stays in front of the people relying on it.

What Sovrinty does

When a record moves to in review, Sovrinty routes it by SME profile: who has the expertise, who is available, and who has room in their workload. The work reaches someone qualified and free to do it, and review begins with the right reviewer already on it.
