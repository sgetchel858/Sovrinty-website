Where it shows up

An answer lands in a board report, a filing, or a chat, and someone acts on it. If you cannot show where it came from and who stood behind it, a confident answer is still a liability.

What Sovrinty does

Only approved sources reach the answer pool, and every claim is cited back to its source. Each question and answer is sealed to an immutable record you cannot edit after the fact. When someone asks how you know an answer was right, that record is your proof.
