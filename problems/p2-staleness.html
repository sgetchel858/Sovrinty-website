Problem P2 · Staleness

“A trusted answer quietly goes stale and nobody notices.”

Approved → Stale

Where it shows up

A policy, rate, or control gets updated upstream, and the approved answers built on it keep circulating as if nothing changed. Your team acts on yesterday’s truth without knowing it, and you find out when a regulator or an executive asks why the answer no longer matches the source.

What Sovrinty does

The Golden Spike links every answer to the sources and expert Q&A it is built on. When a source changes, every answer downstream flips from approved to stale and routes for re-certification. Nobody can act on it until an expert signs off again. You catch staleness the moment it happens.
