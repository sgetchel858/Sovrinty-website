Problem P5 · Access control

“Who’s even allowed to receive this answer?”

Where it shows up

An answer can carry more than its words. Whether a person sends it or an AI agent does, it can deliver just what the recipient was cleared to see, or expose the contents of a file they were never cleared to open. When access is enforced on the document but not on the answer drawn from it, clearance stops at the wrong layer, and the breach is done before anyone notices.

What Sovrinty does

Attribute-based access control runs at the AI layer, gating both retrieval and the answer by each person’s clearance. The right people get the answer they’re entitled to. To everyone else, that answer never existed.
